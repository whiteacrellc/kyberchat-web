Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Our commitment to your privacy and security.

1. Introduction

Welcome to KyberChat, an encrypted communication platform developed and maintained by WhiteAcre Software. This document outlines our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service (TOS) regarding the collection, use, and protection of your data.

2. Encryption & Message Privacy

Security is our foundational principle. KyberChat uses the latest E2E encryption protocols to secure all communications. Because of this architectural design, KyberChat has no ability to read, decrypt, or access users' chat messages under any circumstances. Only the intended recipients of a message hold the keys necessary to decrypt it.

3. Data Collection

By using KyberChat, you agree to our Terms of Service. In order to operate the platform and route messages effectively, KyberChat collects only the minimal information strictly needed to run the service (such as account identifiers and network routing metadata). This operational data does not include the contents of your messages.

4. Infrastructure

To ensure high performance and reliability, KyberChat runs securely on the Google Cloud Platform (GCP). All infrastructure deployments are managed strictly by WhiteAcre Software.

5. Legal Compliance

WhiteAcre Software respects the rule of law. KyberChat will respond to all lawful requests for information from the US Government. However, please note the following limitations regarding our capabilities:

  • WhiteAcre Software will only respond to valid, binding legal requests from US Federal or state governments.
  • Because we cannot read user messages due to our E2E encryption implementation, we can only provide the limited operational and metadata we actually possess.
